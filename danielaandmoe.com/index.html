Our Story

7 years, 7 seas, 7 vows.

7 years ago. Ours wasn't love at first sight. What started with unfettered conversation brewed into deep admiration. Only made stronger over years of seeing each other grow through tough times. Sometimes at the airport, sometimes via texts, and sometimes through stories from our friends.

7 seas apart. Distance was never a barrier for falling in love was fate. Engineered by those who love us without bounds. Our parents: who created "unapproved" online matrimonial profiles. Our friends: who teased us and persisted because they just knew this was meant to be. And all of you: who cheered us along. In someway, you made us who we are today. We wouldn't be here without you!

7 pherre together. A lifetime of the same old boundless love is what we're signing up for next. We're asking you to fly across borders because the vows will feel incomplete without your love & blessings.
