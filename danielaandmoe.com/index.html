Our Story

Ours wasn't love at first sight. It brewed over years of seeing each other grow through life, sometimes at the airport, sometimes on LinkedIn, and other times through stories from our many common friends. The journey from knowing of each other since 2014 to actually getting to know each other in 2023 had many Bollywood twists and turns.

Our parents' "not approved" efforts to find a partner for us, our friends' terribly effective match-making, and our instincts to just send that one text have brought us to where we are. Suffice it to say that this couldn't have happened without all of you, and we can't wait to celebrate with you!
