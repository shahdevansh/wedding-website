Our Story

7 years, 7 oceans, 7 vows.

Ours wasn't love at first sight. We met 7 years back in 2018. What started as mutual admiration brewed over years of seeing each other grow through tough times: Sometimes at the airport, sometimes on LinkedIn, and other times through stories from our many friends. This bond gave us confidence to start a relationship 7 oceans apart and we've only grown closer since. We're asking you to fly across borders as we solidify our commitment into forever with 7 traditional vows.

Because we couldn't be here without love that knows no bounds. Love from our parents: who created "unapproved" online matrimonial profiles. Love from our friends: who teased us and persisted because they just knew this was meant to be. Love from all of you: who have made us who we are today. We can't wait to celebrate with you!

  • What will the weather be like?

    We expect temperatures between 20° to 32°C, but it can feel humid. Pack light, airy and comfortable clothes!

  • What should I wear?

    Please see our Events Schedule for dress codes. And then feel free to follow your heart! Just remember - no stiletto heels for the beach venue.

  • Can I take photos?

    Yes! We would love for you to take photos and share them on our app Joy.

  • Is the venue wheelchair accessible?

    Yes, the celebrations are ~250m away from your room. We will have a golf cart available for anyone needing assistance getting around.
